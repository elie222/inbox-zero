## Key Elements
- **Page**: AI Assistant â†’ Settings tab
- **Purpose**: Configure AI assistant behavior and personalization
- **Setting Types**:

    - **Edit button**: Writing style, About you, Digest
    - **Action button**: Draft knowledge base ("Manage Draft Knowledge"), Email signature ("Set Signature"), Learned Patterns ("View")
