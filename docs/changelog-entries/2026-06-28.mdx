---
description: "Organization Rules"
---

Organization admins can now create AI assistant rules that roll out to every team member automatically. Set policies once from your organization settings — members see them in their rules list and can turn them on or off for their own inbox.

- New Rules tab in organization settings for admins to create, edit, and enable rules across all members
- Managed rules appear on each member's assistant with a "Managed by organization" badge
- Members can toggle organization rules for their account without changing the org-wide definition
