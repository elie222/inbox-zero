---
description: "Reschedule & Cancel Bookings"
---

People who book time with you can now reschedule or cancel directly from their confirmation email — no back-and-forth needed. Both you and your guest receive updated email notifications, and the calendar event is adjusted automatically.

- Reschedule page shows your real-time availability so guests can pick a new slot
- Cancellation support lets guests free up time without messaging you
- When your AI assistant can't send an email on your behalf, the error message now explains why instead of showing a generic failure
