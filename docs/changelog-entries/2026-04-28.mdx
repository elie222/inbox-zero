description: "Redesigned Rule Editor"
---

Creating and editing rules is now cleaner and faster. The rule editor has a streamlined flat layout, collapsible advanced options, and dashed add-row buttons that keep your focus on the conditions and actions that matter.

- AI chat email cards now lead with a numbered summary instead of raw subject lines, making it easier to scan and reference emails across turns
- Assistant test and history tabs show inline subjects grouped by date for quicker browsing
- Rule creation from the assistant chat now flows through the same panel, so you stay in context while building rules
