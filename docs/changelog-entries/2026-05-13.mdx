---
description: "Outlook Assistant and Smarter Scheduling"
---

The assistant now works natively with Outlook folders and categories. If you're on a Microsoft email account, you can ask the assistant to list your folders, move threads into specific folders, or create new ones — all without leaving the chat.

- Scheduling replies now prefer your booking links when someone asks to meet, so contacts get your availability page instead of a manual list of times
- The public booking page is now fully responsive on mobile — calendar, time slots, and timezone picker all fit small screens
- Rule suggestion cards now display descriptive titles like "Product Updates" instead of generic placeholders
