---
description: "Manage Your Chat History"
---

You can now rename and delete chats right from the chat history dropdown. Hover any conversation to reveal a quick menu — rename it to something memorable, or delete it when you're done. Deleted chats are fully redacted so your data stays private.

- Notifications on Slack, Teams, and Telegram now open with emoji prefixes so you can tell the type at a glance — follow-ups, digests, meeting briefs, and more each get their own icon
- Follow-up reminders now clearly indicate whether you're waiting on someone else's reply or need to respond yourself
- Notification previews no longer include quoted reply text, keeping them short and scannable
