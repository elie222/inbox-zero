description: "Subscribe with Apple"
---

You can now subscribe to Inbox Zero Premium directly through the Apple App Store on iOS. Your premium access carries over to the web — sign in on any device and your subscription is already active. If you manage billing through Apple, settings pages guide you to the App Store instead of showing Stripe options.

- Slack draft reply notifications now include direct "Open in Gmail" and "Open in Outlook" links so you can jump straight to the message in your mailbox
