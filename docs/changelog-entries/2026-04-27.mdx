---
description: "Sign In with Apple"
---

You can now sign in to Inbox Zero using your Apple ID. Use Face ID or Touch ID on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac for a fast, private login — then connect your Google or Microsoft mailbox to get started.

- Cleaner bulk unsubscribe notifications — success toasts are now grouped into a single summary instead of one per sender
- The bulk email modal opens instantly instead of waiting on an unnecessary inbox prefetch
