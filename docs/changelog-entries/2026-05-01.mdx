---
description: "Star Messages Automatically"
---

You can now add a star action to your rules so important emails get starred (or flagged in Outlook) the moment they arrive. Pair it with your existing conditions to make sure nothing slips through your inbox unnoticed.

- The Bulk Unsubscriber now shows read rates as visual progress bars, with low-engagement senders highlighted in amber so you can spot unsubscribe candidates at a glance
- Messaging notifications for draft replies now include attachment names, so you know exactly what's queued before a message goes out
- Scheduling replies correctly convert your availability into the sender's timezone, so proposed meeting times always make sense for both sides
