<Update label="March 5, 2026" description="Chat Everywhere">
  Your AI assistant now works across **Teams, Telegram, and Slack** — not just the web app. Send and receive messages, share images, and use slash commands from whichever platform your team already uses.

  - Image attachments in Slack, Telegram, Teams, and web chat
  - Slash commands for Teams and Slack
  - Preview drafts before they send from messaging platforms
  - Unsubscribe directly from assistant chat
</Update>

<Update label="March 3, 2026" description="Draft Confidence & Smarter Scheduling">
  The assistant now shows you how confident it is about each draft before sending. Plus, scheduling suggestions are smarter — they include calendar links and format times as easy-to-scan bullet points.

  - Draft confidence presets so you control how aggressively the assistant sends
  - Calendar-aware scheduling with direct links
  - Reorganized assistant settings
</Update>
