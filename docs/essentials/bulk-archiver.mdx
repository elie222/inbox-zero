## Getting Started

To use this feature, click on the `Bulk Archive` tab in the left sidebar. Or visit [this link](https://www.getinboxzero.com/bulk-archive).

### How To Use

The Bulk Archiver lets you quickly clean up your inbox by archiving large batches of old emails at once.

You can filter emails by sender, date range, or label to find the emails you want to archive, and then archive them all with one click.
