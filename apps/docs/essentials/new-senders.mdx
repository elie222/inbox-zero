## Getting Started

To view your analytics, click on the `New Senders` tab in the left sidebar. Or visit [this link](https://www.getinboxzero.com/new-senders).

### What is a "New Sender"?

A new sender is someone who has emailed you for the first time this week.
This is a great way to keep track of new contacts and potential leads.
It's also a good way to quickly unsubscribe from unwanted emails.
